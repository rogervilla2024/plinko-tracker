Why Isn't Everyone Playing Plinko?

Crash Game Appeal

  • Adrenaline: The tension of "when to cash out"
  • Illusion of control: Players feel they can influence outcomes
  • Social: Watching others win/lose in real-time
  • Near-misses: "I should have cashed out earlier!"

Plinko Reality

  • Passive: No decisions after dropping
  • Less exciting: No tension buildup
  • Faster losses: More drops per hour
  • Isolation: Single-player experience
