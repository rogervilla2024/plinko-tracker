What is RTP?

RTP (Return to Player) is the percentage of all wagered money that a game pays back to players over time.

  • 99% RTP means: For every $100 wagered, $99 is returned on average
  • House Edge: 100% - RTP = 1%
  • The casino keeps 1% of all bets as profit
